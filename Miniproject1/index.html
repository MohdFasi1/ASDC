About Us

Air India has been in operation for over **90 years** and has a workforce of over **12,000 employees** worldwide. The airline operates **79 destinations** and **59 nonstop international routes** ¹. Air India was founded by JRD Tata, who was also the company's first pilot. The airline has many firsts to its credit, including launching India's first commercial airline in 1932 and being the world's first airline with a full jet-engine fleet in 1962 ¹. Air India is the flag carrier of India and has the largest international network and most nonstop routes connecting India with global destinations ¹. In January 2022, Air India was reacquired by Tata Group, India's largest conglomerate ¹.
The airline is committed to operating with net-zero carbon emissions by 2050 ¹

Frequently Asked Questions

Q. How do I make a flight booking on Flight booking website?
A: You can book a flight on Flight booking website in five easy steps: Head over to the Flight booking website flight booking page, Enter your departure and arrival destinations, Select your air travel dates, Choose from our wide range of cheap flights based on your airfare preferences, Click on ‘Book Now’ and your air flight booking is done. Alternatively, you can also use the Flight booking website app for your flight ticket booking. Download the Flight booking website app, Put in the details i.e. date of journey, departure and arrival destinations, travel class of your choice, Select on your best comfortable option and click on 'Book Now'.

Q - Can I avail domestic flight offers on Flight booking website?
A: Of course, you can. While making domestic flight bookings, you can avail any special offer that is active at that time. In accordance with the offer selected, a listing of eligible cheapest flights would show up on your screen. You can then apply the price filter and click on the downwards arrow, following which budget-friendly flights would start showing up in ascending order from the top (lowest price on top).

Q - How can I avail budget air tickets on Flight booking website?
A: It’s super-easy to avail budget airfare while booking your cheap flight tickets on Flight booking website. Just select the ‘Price’ filter once the available flight options are displayed and adjust according to your convenience. On the Flight booking website, you can select the downward arrow, which will show the lowest airfare at the top and continue downward in ascending order.
